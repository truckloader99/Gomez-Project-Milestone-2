About Us

I’m Dylan Gomez, the lead advocate behind Drop For Drop. I turn information into stories that drive understanding and equity. What started as a school project quickly grew into a movement which is now one that blends data, storytelling, and a push for change.

My goal is to build tools and stories that inspire action; one drop at a time. Drop For Drop is a student advocacy group committed to raising awareness about issues about water hygiene and promoting sustainable solutions. We believe that access to clean water is a human right, and we work to raise voices from underserved communities.

Our journey began in 2025 as a school project in Mindanao, where we saw in person the challenges families face in accessing safe water.
