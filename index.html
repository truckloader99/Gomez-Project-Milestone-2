Clean Water is a Right, not a Privilege

Access to safe water is essential for life, yet millions are denied this basic human right every day.





SDG 6 aims to ensure availability and sustainable management of water and sanitation for all by 2030.
It addresses water scarcity, pollution, and the need for improved infrastructure and hygiene practices worldwide.
SDG 6 is not just about water — it’s about dignity, health, and opportunity for every person on the planet.
